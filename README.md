## About

I am a **CAE Engineer** at **Toyota Motor Corporation** (Japan) and a passionate researcher in computational mechanics. My work focuses on computer-aided engineering and simulation for automotive development — structural, thermal, and durability analysis using the finite element method.

Beyond industry, I actively pursue academic research in numerical methods (FEM, meshfree, and isogeometric analysis), physics-informed neural networks (PINNs), inverse problems, and biomechanics.

- **Role:** CAE Engineer @ Toyota Motor Corporation
- **Research:** meshfree methods · FGM plates · bio-inspired composites · PINNs
- **Website:** https://ntthanh116.github.io
- **Contact:** thanh.nguyen116engineer@gmail.com
